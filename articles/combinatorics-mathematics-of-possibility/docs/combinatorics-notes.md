# Combinatorics Notes

Combinatorics studies structured possibility.

## Core questions

- What objects are being counted?
- Does order matter?
- Is repetition allowed?
- Are objects labeled or unlabeled?
- Are there constraints?
- Are cases disjoint or overlapping?
- Are symmetries identified?
- Is the count being interpreted as probability?
- Does the possibility space reflect the real system responsibly?

## Core structures

- permutations;
- combinations;
- binomial coefficients;
- inclusion-exclusion;
- pigeonhole principle;
- recurrences;
- generating functions;
- graph counts;
- partitions and distributions;
- search spaces.
