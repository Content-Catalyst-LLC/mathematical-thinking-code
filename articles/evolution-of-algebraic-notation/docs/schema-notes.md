# Schema Notes

The SQL schema models notation styles, milestones, symbol records, expression examples, transformation rules, and notation warnings.

## Core tables

- `notation_style`: major styles such as procedural, rhetorical, syncopated, symbolic, structural, logical, computational.
- `notation_milestone`: historical developments and figures.
- `symbol_record`: symbol meanings, contexts, roles, ambiguity, and pedagogy notes.
- `expression_example`: expression texts and historical layers.
- `transformation_rule`: symbolic transformation metadata and side conditions.
- `notation_warning`: risks around history, pedagogy, domain assumptions, computing, and access.

## Professional use cases

- history of mathematics teaching;
- algebra education;
- symbolic algebra literacy;
- expression parser design;
- computer algebra documentation;
- mathematical notation audits;
- accessible mathematical communication.
