# Validation Plan

## Dataset checks

- Confirm every milestone references a valid notation style.
- Confirm every symbol has a meaning context and ambiguity note.
- Confirm every transformation rule has a mathematical condition.
- Confirm warnings include historical, pedagogical, domain, and computing risks.

## Expression checks

- Confirm expression-tree outputs are valid.
- Confirm polynomial identity tables are generated.
- Confirm finite checks are labeled as illustrative, not proof.
- Confirm Graphviz DOT output is syntactically safe.

## Historical review

- Check primary and secondary sources before publishing detailed historical claims.
- Avoid overgeneralizing from modern notation.
- Distinguish original notation from modern reconstruction.
- Include global traditions and avoid Eurocentric simplification.

## Responsible-use note

This scaffold is for structured mathematical and historical reflection. It does not replace specialist historiography, primary-source scholarship, formal proof, symbolic algebra systems, or accessibility review.
