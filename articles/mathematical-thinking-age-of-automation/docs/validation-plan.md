# Validation Plan

## Dataset checks

- Confirm every automation task includes assumptions and a verification method.
- Confirm every tool includes strength, human review, and failure mode.
- Confirm every verification record includes evidence standard and trust boundary.
- Confirm every risk includes a mitigation.
- Confirm every human judgment skill includes a review question.
- Confirm proof-assistant layers separate human and machine roles.

## Mathematical review

- Distinguish calculation, symbolic transformation, simulation, AI explanation, and formal proof.
- Distinguish proof from model validation.
- Distinguish AI proposal from mathematical authority.
- Distinguish formal verification from intended-meaning review.
- Distinguish optimization success from responsible use.

## Responsible-use note

This scaffold is for structured mathematical automation review. It does not replace mathematical proof, formal verification, numerical analysis, software testing, model validation, classroom assessment, peer review, or ethical review.
