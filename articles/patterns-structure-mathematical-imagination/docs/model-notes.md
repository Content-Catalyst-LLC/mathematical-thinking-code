# Model Notes

This article treats mathematical imagination as disciplined structural perception.

The companion repository models four transitions:

1. **Pattern to sequence** — observed regularity becomes tabular, recursive, or symbolic data.
2. **Sequence to structure** — repeated behavior is interpreted through recurrence, transformation, or graph form.
3. **Structure to invariant** — properties preserved under transformation are identified and compared.
4. **Imagination to proof discipline** — conjectures are tested through counterexamples, edge cases, and dependency-aware reasoning.

The repository intentionally separates observation, representation, invariant computation, and proof interpretation. This prevents a common mathematical error: mistaking visible pattern or computational output for proof.
