# Induction Notes

Induction is the proof counterpart of recursion.

## Common proof types

- ordinary induction;
- strong induction;
- structural induction;
- invariant proofs;
- recursive correctness proofs.

## Audit questions

- Is the base case correct?
- Does the inductive step use the hypothesis correctly?
- Does the proof cover all cases?
- Is the recursive structure well-founded?
- Are boundary cases checked?
