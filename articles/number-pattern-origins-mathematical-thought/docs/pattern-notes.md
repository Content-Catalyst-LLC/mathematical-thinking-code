# Pattern Notes

Patterns may be arithmetic, quadratic, geometric, recursive, cyclic, visual, rhythmic, statistical, or structural.

## Pattern audit questions

- What is the observed pattern?
- What finite evidence supports it?
- What rule is proposed?
- What proof status is attached to the rule?
- Could a counterexample exist?
- Is the pattern exact, approximate, symbolic, cultural, or statistical?
- What context is omitted by the representation?

## Key distinction

Detected pattern is not the same as understood structure. Proof, context, and interpretation matter.
