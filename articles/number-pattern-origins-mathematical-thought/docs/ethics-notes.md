# Ethics Notes

Number and pattern compress reality. This can clarify, but it can also distort.

## Responsible questions

- What is counted?
- What is excluded?
- Who defined the categories?
- What pattern is being claimed?
- What evidence supports the pattern?
- Is the pattern causal, structural, statistical, symbolic, or accidental?
- What decisions may be influenced by the number or pattern?
- Which communities are affected by the interpretation?

Number and pattern should make reality more accountable, not less.
