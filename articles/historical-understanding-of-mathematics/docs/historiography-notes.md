# Historiography Notes

The historical understanding of mathematics studies how mathematical ideas become thinkable, writable, teachable, transferable, authoritative, and usable.

## Core distinction

Mathematical validity is not the same as the history of mathematical practice.

## Responsible questions

- What object of thought is being studied?
- What medium represents it?
- What method creates authority?
- What does the practice mean in context?
- What is original expression and what is modern reconstruction?
- Whose work is visible or invisible in the canon?
