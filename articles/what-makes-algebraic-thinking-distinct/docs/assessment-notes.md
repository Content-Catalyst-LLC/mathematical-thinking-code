# Algebraic Learning Assessment Notes

Assessment of algebraic thinking should include more than procedural accuracy.

## Dimensions to assess

- variable-role interpretation;
- relational understanding of equality;
- expression/equation distinction;
- transformation validity;
- function dependence;
- proof or justification of general claims;
- verification of candidate solutions;
- explanation of structure.

## Useful prompts

- What role does the variable play here?
- Are these two expressions equivalent? How do you know?
- What transformation did you use, and what does it preserve?
- Does your candidate solution satisfy the original equation?
- Is this equation a condition, an identity, a function relation, or a model?
