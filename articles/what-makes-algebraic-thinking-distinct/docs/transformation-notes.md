# Transformation Notes

Algebraic transformation is not symbol movement. It is meaning-preserving rewriting under conditions.

## Safe or usually safe transformations

- adding the same expression to both sides of an equation;
- expanding via distributivity;
- factoring when the coefficient domain supports the factorization;
- substituting equal expressions for equal expressions.

## Risky transformations

- dividing by an expression that may be zero;
- squaring both sides without checking extraneous solutions;
- taking square roots without sign and domain checks;
- canceling symbols across addition;
- simplifying expressions while changing domain.

## Verification habit

Candidate solutions should be substituted into the original equation or checked through a residual.
