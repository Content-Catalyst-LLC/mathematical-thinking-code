# Validation Plan

## Dataset checks

- Confirm every discovery candidate has an output type, assumptions, status, and interpretation question.
- Confirm every evaluator has a criterion and limitation.
- Confirm every verification record has an evidence standard and remaining question.
- Confirm every risk has a mitigation.
- Confirm every interpretation record includes novelty, significance, proof status, and workflow credit notes.
- Confirm every workflow stage separates AI role, evaluator role, and human role.
- Confirm proof-status categories distinguish testing, proof, machine checking, and rejection.

## Mathematical review

- Distinguish generated example from theorem.
- Distinguish conjecture from proof.
- Distinguish proof sketch from verified proof.
- Distinguish formal statement from intended theorem.
- Distinguish finite testing from universal proof.
- Distinguish novelty from mathematical significance.

## Responsible-use note

This scaffold is for structured AI-assisted discovery review. It does not replace mathematical proof, formal verification, expert review, literature review, theorem-prover checking, numerical validation, software testing, or ethical review.
