# Ethics and Credit Notes

AI-assisted mathematical discovery distributes labor across humans, systems, datasets, evaluators, libraries, and proof processes.

## Responsible documentation

- tool or model used;
- prompts or objectives where appropriate;
- generated candidates;
- evaluator design;
- datasets, libraries, and formal environments;
- human review and proof labor;
- verification status;
- novelty and literature review;
- credit and workflow notes.

Mathematical discovery should remain transparent, reproducible, and responsibly credited.
