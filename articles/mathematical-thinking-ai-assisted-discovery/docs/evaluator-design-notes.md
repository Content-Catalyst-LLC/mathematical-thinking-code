# Evaluator Design Notes

Evaluator design determines what an AI-assisted discovery workflow can actually validate.

## Review questions

- What candidate type is being evaluated?
- What criterion is being optimized or checked?
- Does the criterion capture the mathematical problem?
- Could the system overfit the evaluator?
- Are there held-out cases or alternative evaluators?
- Does the evaluator test truth, performance, structure, or only a proxy?

Evaluator quality shapes discovery quality.
