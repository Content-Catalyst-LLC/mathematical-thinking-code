# Validation Plan

## Dataset checks

- Confirm every structure has objects, operations, laws, and preservation map.
- Confirm every foundation view has a strength and limitation.
- Confirm every formal system has language, axioms, inference rules, intended use, and limitation.
- Confirm every model interpretation has assumption risks and responsible questions.
- Confirm every proof-assistant layer separates human and machine roles.
- Confirm abstraction warnings include mitigations.

## Mathematical review

- Distinguish objects from representations.
- Distinguish proof from model interpretation.
- Distinguish formal correctness from ethical adequacy.
- Distinguish preservation from total sameness.
- Distinguish computation from proof or wisdom.

## Responsible-use note

This scaffold is for structured mathematical and philosophical reflection. It does not replace mathematical proof, formal verification, expert model validation, philosophy of mathematics, ethics review, or affected-community review where applicable.
