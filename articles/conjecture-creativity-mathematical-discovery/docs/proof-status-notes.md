# Proof-Status Notes

Proof-status discipline is central to responsible mathematical discovery.

## Suggested statuses

- observed
- conjectured
- supported by finite evidence
- supported by simulation
- refuted by counterexample
- proved
- proved under assumptions
- formalized
- formalization pending
- open

## Required distinction

Finite evidence, simulation, and computational search can suggest or stress-test a conjecture. They do not replace proof unless the claim is explicitly finite and the search is exhaustive, verified, and correctly specified.
