# Diagram Commutativity Notes

A categorical diagram should state a mathematical condition.

## Review questions

- What are the objects?
- What are the arrows?
- Which paths are being compared?
- What equality of composites is asserted?
- What universal property, if any, is encoded?
- Does the diagram carry proof-bearing content or only decoration?

A commutative diagram is a compact statement about equality of composed morphisms.
