# Responsible Abstraction Notes

Category-level abstraction can clarify structure, but it can also hide important details.

## Questions

- What does the abstraction preserve?
- What does it forget?
- What concrete examples justify the abstraction?
- What implementation costs are hidden?
- What social, ecological, institutional, or contextual realities are excluded?
- Who can inspect and challenge the model?

Formal structure is not complete reality.
