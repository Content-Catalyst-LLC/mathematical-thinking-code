# Diagram Notes

Diagrams are mathematical representations, not merely illustrations.

## Diagram audit questions

- What object or relation does the diagram represent?
- Which features are essential?
- Which features are accidental artifacts of the drawing?
- Is the diagram to scale?
- Does the diagram represent a general case or a special case?
- What would be needed to turn the visual insight into proof?
- What does the diagram omit?

## Responsible use

Use diagrams to explore, communicate, and organize mathematical reasoning, but require definitions, assumptions, and proof for conclusions.
