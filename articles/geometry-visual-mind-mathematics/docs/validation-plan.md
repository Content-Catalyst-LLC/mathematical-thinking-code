# Validation Plan

## Geometric computation

- Check distances against coordinate inputs.
- Check triangle area through determinant formula.
- Check unit-circle membership by residual.
- Check orientation sign and magnitude.

## Transformation audits

- Identify whether transformation is isometry, similarity, projection, or topological deformation.
- Record what changes and what is preserved.
- Flag scaling and projection as non-distance-preserving where appropriate.
- Record transformation center, axis, scale, or coordinate convention where needed.

## Diagram and visualization audits

- Record whether diagrams are to scale.
- Distinguish graph layout from graph structure.
- State what projections preserve and omit.
- Treat visual evidence as exploratory unless proof or validated computation is supplied.

## Responsible-use note

Visualizations and computational geometry outputs should be interpreted within mathematical definitions, modeling assumptions, and domain context.
