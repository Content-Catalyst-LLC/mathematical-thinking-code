# Validation Plan

## Dataset checks

- Confirm every model has type, purpose, target system, intended use, and scope limit.
- Confirm every assumption has type and failure consequence.
- Confirm every variable or parameter has role, unit, and uncertainty note.
- Confirm every calibration record lists method, data, risk, and review question.
- Confirm every validation record lists evidence, limitation, and credibility note.
- Confirm uncertainty sources are typed separately.
- Confirm sensitivity records connect tested factors to decision relevance.
- Confirm responsible modeling checks cover representation, relationship, testing, revision, and responsibility.

## Mathematical and scientific review

- Distinguish model from reality.
- Distinguish calibration from validation.
- Distinguish verification from validation.
- Distinguish prediction from explanation.
- Distinguish scenario from forecast.
- Distinguish uncertainty from ignorance.
- Distinguish decision support from decision authority.

## Responsible-use note

This scaffold is for structured scientific modeling review. It does not replace scientific expertise, empirical validation, formal verification, software testing, peer review, uncertainty analysis, domain review, policy judgment, or ethical review.
