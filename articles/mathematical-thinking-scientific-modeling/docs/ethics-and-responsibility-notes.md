# Ethics and Responsibility Notes

Scientific models influence decisions and can affect public life, infrastructure, ecosystems, health, and institutions.

## Responsible questions

- Whose reality is represented?
- Whose risk is visible?
- Whose uncertainty matters?
- Who can inspect or challenge the model?
- How will outputs be used?
- What values or tradeoffs are hidden in assumptions?
- What harms could follow from misuse?

Models should support responsible judgment, not replace it.
