# Centrality Notes

Centrality measures summarize structural position.

## Cautions

- Degree is not the same as authority.
- Betweenness may indicate brokerage, burden, or vulnerability.
- Closeness is difficult in disconnected graphs.
- Eigenvector-style measures can reinforce concentration.
- Centrality depends on edge meaning and data quality.

Centrality is a structural summary, not a moral or institutional judgment.
