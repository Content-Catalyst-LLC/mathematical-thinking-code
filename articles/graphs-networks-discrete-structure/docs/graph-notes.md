# Graph Notes

A graph is a discrete structure made from vertices and edges.

## Core questions

- What are the vertices?
- What do edges mean?
- Is the graph directed or undirected?
- Are weights present?
- Are loops or multiple edges allowed?
- Is the graph connected?
- Which paths exist?
- What does centrality actually measure?
- What should not be inferred from the graph?

A graph drawing is not the graph itself. The graph is the mathematical structure defined by its vertex set and edge set.
