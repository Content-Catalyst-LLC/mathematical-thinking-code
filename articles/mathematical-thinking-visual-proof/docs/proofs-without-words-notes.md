# Proofs Without Words Notes

Proofs without words are visual invitations to reconstruct an argument.

## Review questions

- What is being counted, rearranged, compared, or preserved?
- What are the missing words?
- Does the visual pattern hold for arbitrary cases?
- What assumptions are implicit?
- Can the visual argument be translated into a symbolic or verbal proof?

A proof without words is strongest when the viewer can supply the general argument.
