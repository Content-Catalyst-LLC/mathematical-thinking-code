# Diagram Relation Notes

A mathematical diagram is not just an image. It is a representation of objects and relations.

## Review questions

- What visual feature is doing the mathematical work?
- What relation does the feature represent?
- Is the relation structural or accidental?
- Can the diagram be varied without losing the relation?
- What theorem, definition, or invariant justifies the relation?
- Can the argument be expressed verbally, symbolically, or formally?
