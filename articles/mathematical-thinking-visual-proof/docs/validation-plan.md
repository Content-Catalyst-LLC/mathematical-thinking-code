# Validation Plan

## Dataset checks

- Confirm every visual proof record has a domain, role, proof status, and generalization question.
- Confirm every diagram relation has a visual feature, mathematical relation, and proof requirement.
- Confirm every visual risk has a mitigation.
- Confirm every accessibility review has an alternative description.
- Confirm every visual workflow stage has a question, failure mode, and mitigation.
- Confirm every proof-without-words example includes a missing-words prompt.

## Mathematical review

- Distinguish illustration from evidence, heuristic, argument, and formal proof.
- Distinguish a diagram from the mathematical structure represented.
- Distinguish visual plausibility from proof.
- Distinguish finite patterns from general theorems.
- Distinguish graph layout from abstract graph structure.
- Distinguish visual intuition from accessible explanation.

## Responsible-use note

This scaffold is for structured visual-proof review. It does not replace mathematical proof, formal verification, expert review, accessibility review, classroom judgment, or ethical visualization practice.
