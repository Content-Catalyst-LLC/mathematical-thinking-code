# Validation Plan

## Reasoning metadata

- Confirm every reasoning move has a stage, guiding question, and evidence of learning.
- Confirm every task includes both algorithmic and non-algorithmic components.
- Confirm assessment dimensions have explicit max scores.
- Confirm solution audits can be summarized without exposing sensitive personal data.

## AI-output verification

- Check residuals where numerical answers are supplied.
- Check domains for formula use.
- Distinguish examples, conjectures, proof sketches, and verified proofs.
- Require interpretation and assumption review.

## Education research caution

Synthetic examples in this repository are for methods demonstration and curriculum design. Real student work requires consent, privacy protection, contextual interpretation, and careful validation.
