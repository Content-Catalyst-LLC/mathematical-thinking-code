# AI Verification Notes

AI and computational tools can support mathematical learning, but tool output should be verified.

## Verification questions

- What problem was actually solved?
- What assumptions were made?
- Does the answer satisfy the original equation, claim, or model?
- Is the domain valid?
- Is the output a proof, a conjecture, a finite check, or an explanation?
- Can the learner explain the method?
- What would count as a counterexample?

## Responsible practice

AI-generated mathematics should be treated as a draft or candidate unless checked through proof, computation, residual checks, domain review, or expert judgment.
