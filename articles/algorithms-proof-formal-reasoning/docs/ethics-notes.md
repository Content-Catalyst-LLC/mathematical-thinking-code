# Ethics Notes

Formal correctness is necessary but not sufficient.

## Responsible questions

- Is the specification legitimate?
- Who defined the objective?
- What assumptions are hidden?
- What preconditions fail in practice?
- What categories or priorities are encoded?
- What does the output mean?
- Who is affected by errors?
- What remains outside the proof?

Formal reasoning should make responsibility more visible, not hide it behind technical certainty.
