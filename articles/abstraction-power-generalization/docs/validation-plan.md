# Validation Plan

## Equivalence classes

- Confirm that rational normalization enforces nonzero denominators.
- Verify sign normalization places the denominator in positive form.
- Confirm equivalent rational representatives collapse to the same reduced pair.

## Graph invariants

- Check edge-list parsing.
- Validate degree sequence, vertex count, edge count, component count, and cycle-rank outputs.
- Treat degree sequence as a partial invariant, not a complete isomorphism classifier.

## Generalization workflows

- Distinguish finite-case support from proof.
- Link counterexamples to specific claims.
- Track domain and required assumptions explicitly.

## Structure-preserving maps

- Audit which operations are preserved.
- Record when a map preserves one relation but not another.
- Avoid treating visual or symbolic resemblance as structural equivalence unless the relevant relations are preserved.

## Interpretation

The repository is for mathematical exploration, reproducible documentation, and professional reasoning support. It does not replace proof, peer review, formal verification, or domain-specific mathematical expertise.
