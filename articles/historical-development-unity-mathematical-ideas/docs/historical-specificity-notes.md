# Historical Specificity Notes

The unity of mathematical ideas should not flatten mathematical history.

## Responsible practices

- Distinguish modern reconstruction from original notation.
- Respect procedural, diagrammatic, tabular, oral, material, and commentarial forms of reasoning.
- Avoid treating proof as the only legitimate mathematical practice.
- Avoid reducing global traditions to precursors of modern European notation.
- Recognize translation, teaching, instruments, craft, and collective labor.
