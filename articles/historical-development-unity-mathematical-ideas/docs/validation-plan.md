# Validation Plan

## Dataset checks

- Confirm every mathematical idea has a representation, transformation, and invariant.
- Confirm every cross-field connection has a caution note.
- Confirm transformation/invariant pairs specify what is preserved.
- Confirm proof, algorithm, model, simulation, and verification remain distinct evidence standards.
- Confirm warnings include mitigation steps.

## Mathematical review

- Distinguish formal similarity from contextual sameness.
- Distinguish proof from model validation.
- Distinguish computation from explanation or wisdom.
- Distinguish structural preservation from total equivalence.
- Distinguish historical reconstruction from original practice.

## Responsible-use note

This scaffold supports structured reflection on mathematical unity. It does not replace mathematical proof, history of mathematics scholarship, domain-specific validation, formal verification, ethical review, or affected-community review where applicable.
