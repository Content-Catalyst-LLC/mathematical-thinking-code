# Mapping Ethics

Sets, relations, and functions can shape real-world systems.

## Responsible questions

- Who or what is included in the set?
- Who or what is excluded?
- What relation is being claimed?
- What does equivalence preserve and ignore?
- What makes one object rank above another?
- What input domain was the mapping designed for?
- What outputs are possible?
- What happens if the model is applied outside its domain?
- Which affected groups experience the consequences?

Mathematical structure should clarify assumptions, not hide judgment behind formal notation.
