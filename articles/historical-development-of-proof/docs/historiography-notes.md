# Historiography Notes

The history of proof should not be reduced to a single linear triumphal narrative.

## Responsible principles

- Include multiple mathematical traditions.
- Preserve distinctions among proof styles.
- Avoid judging all past mathematics by modern formal standards.
- Attend to media: tablets, papyri, diagrams, commentary, print, journals, code.
- Recognize translation, canon formation, and institutional power.
- Treat machine-checked proof as a new chapter, not the end of proof history.
