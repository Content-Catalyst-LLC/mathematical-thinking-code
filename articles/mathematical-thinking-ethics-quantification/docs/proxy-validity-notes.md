# Proxy Validity Notes

Many metrics are proxies for deeper values.

## Review questions

- What target value or concept is the proxy meant to represent?
- How strong is the proxy-target relationship?
- Does the relationship hold across groups, contexts, and time?
- Can the proxy be gamed?
- What does the proxy omit?
- What qualitative or contextual evidence should accompany it?

A proxy may approximate a value, but the proxy is not the value itself.
