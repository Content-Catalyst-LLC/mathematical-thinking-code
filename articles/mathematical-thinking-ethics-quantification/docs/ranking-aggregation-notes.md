# Ranking and Aggregation Notes

Aggregation summarizes. Ranking orders. Both can clarify, and both can hide.

## Review questions

- What method combines observations or indicators?
- What is lost in aggregation?
- What subgroup or distributional analysis is needed?
- How stable is the ranking under alternative weights or methods?
- Are small differences being exaggerated?
- What context is erased by ordinal comparison?

A composite score or rank should not replace interpretation.
