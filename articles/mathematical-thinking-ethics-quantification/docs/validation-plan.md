# Validation Plan

## Dataset checks

- Confirm every metric has type, target concept, proxy/method, consequence level, intended use, and invalid use warning.
- Confirm every risk has problem and mitigation.
- Confirm every validity review includes construct validity, uncertainty, subgroup review, and context.
- Confirm every governance check includes documentation, contestability, audit frequency, and invalid use.
- Confirm Goodhart records connect metric, goal, optimization pressure, distortion, and countermeasure.
- Confirm aggregation reviews state what is shown, hidden, and disaggregation needed.
- Confirm ranking reviews state instability source and responsible reporting.
- Confirm uncertainty reviews include a communication method.
- Confirm justice reviews include recognition, distribution, voice, and harm questions.
- Confirm responsible quantification checks cover define, measure, contextualize, govern, and justice.

## Ethical and mathematical review

- Distinguish measurement from proxy.
- Distinguish proxy from value.
- Distinguish score from complete judgment.
- Distinguish ranking from stable ordering.
- Distinguish precision from certainty.
- Distinguish indicator from goal.
- Distinguish decision support from decision authority.

## Responsible-use note

This scaffold is for structured quantification ethics review. It does not replace domain expertise, empirical validation, community review, legal review, ethical review, qualitative evidence, institutional accountability, or public deliberation.
