# Quantification Notes

Quantification turns concepts, events, risks, behaviors, harms, benefits, or values into numbers.

## Review questions

- What concept is being quantified?
- What method turns the concept into a number?
- Is the number a measurement, proxy, indicator, score, ranking, risk score, or benchmark?
- What uncertainty is present?
- What is the intended use?
- What uses would be invalid?
- Who may be affected by the metric?

A number is not self-explanatory. It is a representation under assumptions.
