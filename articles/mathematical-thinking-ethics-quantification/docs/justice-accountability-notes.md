# Justice and Accountability Notes

Metrics become ethically consequential when they govern access, recognition, resources, risk, or punishment.

## Questions

- Who is visible in the data?
- Who is missing?
- Who benefits from the metric?
- Who may be harmed?
- Who can inspect the method?
- Who can contest errors or misuse?
- What structural conditions does the metric ignore?

Quantification can reveal injustice, but it can also hide or reproduce it.
