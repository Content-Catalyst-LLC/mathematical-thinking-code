# Definition Design Notes

Definitions are design decisions in proof-assistant mathematics.

## Review questions

- Is this a property, subtype, bundled structure, or unbundled predicate?
- Is equality the right notion of sameness, or should isomorphism be used?
- Does the definition support reuse?
- Does the definition preserve mathematical meaning?
- Does the definition make proof scripts clearer or more fragile?
- Does the definition work well with automation?

Definitions are infrastructure for formal proof.
