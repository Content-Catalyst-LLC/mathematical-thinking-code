# Education Notes

Proof assistants can support proof education when they connect formal discipline to mathematical meaning.

## Benefits

- goals and hypotheses become visible;
- invalid steps are rejected;
- assumptions must be explicit;
- induction and rewriting become concrete;
- proof can be explored interactively.

## Risks

- syntax can overwhelm meaning;
- students may follow tactics without understanding;
- formal correctness may be mistaken for explanation;
- examples and diagrams may be neglected.

Proof assistants should supplement, not replace, mathematical conversation and explanation.
