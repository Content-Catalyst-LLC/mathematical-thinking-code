# Validation Plan

## Dataset checks

- Confirm every proof-assistant system has a foundation or logic and trust note.
- Confirm every formalization project has purpose and domain.
- Confirm every theorem statement audit separates informal statement, formal summary, hypotheses, intended meaning review, and risk.
- Confirm every trust boundary includes a review question.
- Confirm every proof layer separates human and machine roles.
- Confirm every AI workflow includes risk and mitigation.
- Confirm responsible verification checks cover define, state, prove, check, and interpret.

## Mathematical review

- Distinguish informal proof from formal proof.
- Distinguish machine checking from mathematical explanation.
- Distinguish formal statement from intended meaning.
- Distinguish tactic success from proof understanding.
- Distinguish formal verification from responsible use.

## Responsible-use note

This scaffold is for structured proof-assistant literacy and formalization review. It does not replace proof-assistant checking, mathematical proof, expert formalization, theorem-prover expertise, software verification, peer review, or ethical review.
