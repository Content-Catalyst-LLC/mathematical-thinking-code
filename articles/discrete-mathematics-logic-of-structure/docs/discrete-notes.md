# Discrete Mathematics Notes

Discrete mathematics studies distinct units organized by relations, rules, and structures.

## Core objects

- finite and countable sets;
- propositions and logical formulas;
- relations and functions;
- graphs and trees;
- sequences and recurrences;
- modular systems;
- Boolean structures;
- algorithms and finite-state systems;
- combinatorial arrangements.

## Core habits

- define the domain;
- distinguish examples from proof;
- track edge cases;
- avoid double-counting;
- check graph structure separately from graph layout;
- document assumptions and invariants.
